E-UNITED

We're in this together!


Being bullied makes kids feel different, powerless, unpopular and alone. It can be difficult to stand up for yourself when you are being bullied, and sometimes it feels like you are all alone and have no idea what to do! E-UNITED is a website that provides such students with support and counselling, as well as the ability to report their bullies anonymously to their respective schools. E-UNITED also has counselling sessions as groups so that children know that there are others facing similiar issues, and that they're never alone during tough times.
